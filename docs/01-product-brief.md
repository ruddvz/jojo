# 01 — Product brief

## User
Rudrakumar Patel (Rudra). One user. Mobile-first (iPhone). Based in London, Ontario; commute range about 25–40 km.

## The job
Rudra is running an active job search with two goals at once:
1. **Income** — land a well-paying job soon.
2. **Immigration** — prioritise roles that support a Spousal Open Work Permit (SOWP) for his wife and a future Permanent Residency (PR) pathway. Eligibility is driven by Canada's NOC/TEER system. See `docs/06-noc-and-immigration.md`.

## What the app is
A personal command centre for the daily hunt. It is NOT a job board and NOT an auto-applier. It helps him:
- Keep every job in one pipeline with a clear status.
- Instantly see which jobs advance immigration (Track A / Track B) and which are income-only.
- For each job, get a **tailored résumé PDF** and a **ready cover letter**, then tap through to apply.
- Mark a job applied so it leaves the active list and is never shown again.
- Run a light **daily routine** and chase stale applications.

## Communication style to honour in copy
Rudra is brief and direct. The UI copy should be plain, confident, and uncluttered. No marketing language.

## Non-goals
- No account system, no cloud sync, no analytics, no ads.
- No scraping of job boards, no automated applications (not possible and not desired). New jobs are added by paste or by hand.
