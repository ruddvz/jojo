# 08 — Cover letters

Generated client-side. Always **shown on screen** and **one-tap copyable**.

## Rules (non-negotiable)
- 3–4 short paragraphs, under 250 words.
- No em dashes anywhere.
- No AI filler (passionate, dynamic, leverage, synergy, excited to contribute, etc.).
- Canadian English spelling (colour, organise, fulfil, modelling, labour, centre).
- No bullet points, no bold, no headings in the body.
- **Never mention immigration, SOWP, or work-permit status.**
- Never mention Order Picker or Walkie Rider certifications unless the job requires them.

## Structure
- Para 1: role + employer + one sharp reason he fits.
- Para 2: most relevant experience with one or two concrete examples; mirror the posting's language.
- Para 3: education or a second supporting point.
- Para 4: short close, available to discuss, thanks.
